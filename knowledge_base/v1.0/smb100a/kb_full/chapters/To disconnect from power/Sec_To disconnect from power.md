# To disconnect from power
The R&S SMB is in the standby state.

NOTICE! Risk of data loss. If you disconnect the product from power when it is in the ready state, you can lose settings and data. Shut it down first.

Set the toggle switch on the power supply to position [0].

The LED of the [ON/STANDBY] key is switched off.

Disconnect the R&S SMB from the power source.
