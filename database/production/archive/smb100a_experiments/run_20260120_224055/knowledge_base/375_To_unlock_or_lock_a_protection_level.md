# To_unlock_or_lock_a_protection_level

To unlock or lock a protection level

In the "Password" entry field, enter the password for the corresponding protection level.

Confirm with the [Enter] key. The checkbox of the protection level is disabled, i.e. the protection is unlocked.

To lock a protection level again, select the checkbox.
